Gather From Near or Far

Upcoming Events and Meetups Join Our Virtual Gatherings and Club Activities Our Hedgehog Meetups page is dedicated to helping you organize, join, and enjoy gatherings with fellow “quill clubbers.” Whether you're planning a casual local meet-and-greet, a virtual webinar with hedgehog experts, or a hedgehog costume party (yes, those are a thing!), we're here to make it easy. If you are unsure where to start, we would reccomend a virtual meet-and-greet. This has been a favorite of many quill clubbers allowing the sharing of funy moments and tips and tricks for all temperments of hedgehog. contact us here to see what other fun activities may be planned, or that you would like to host! Let's build lasting friendships and create a supportive, vibrant community—one quill at a time!

Pipoca

Meet Miss Pipoca! She is a two year old white African Pygmy Hedgehog with snowflake patterned quills. Her favorite pastimes include napping, running on her wheel, eating bugs, exploring everywhere, and attacking hair. Pipoca has a sassy can do attitude with a stubborn streak. She is very set in her ways of how her cage needs to be arranged and will fix it if it is not perfect. Pipoca loves to meet new friends and watch action shows.
