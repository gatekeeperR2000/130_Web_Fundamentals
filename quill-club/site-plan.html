Overview

Purpose

To create a global community dedicated to the love and understanding of hedgehogs, providing educational resources, fostering connections among enthusiasts, and promoting responsible hedgehog care.

Audience

The Quill Club is for anyone who shares a passion for hedgehogs. Whether you're a seasoned hedgehog owner, a curious newcomer, or simply an admirer of these delightful creatures, you'll find a welcoming space to learn, share, and connect with fellow hedgehog lovers.
